Frequently Asked Questions

Where do sessions happen?
Online (Zoom/Meet) or in-person at public libraries across Vaughan (e.g., Civic Centre Resource Library, Bathurst Clark). We choose quiet tables and keep a whiteboard kit handy for diagrams.
Do you give homework?
Yes—10–20 minutes of targeted practice after each session. We use spaced repetition and interleaving so students review past skills while adding new ones. Parents get a note with exactly what to do.
How do payments work?
e-Transfer after each session or monthly bundles for savings. Cancellations are free with 24h notice; inside 24h we try to rebook your slot during the same week.
Can you help with school assignments?
We build understanding and strategy. Students write their own work. We provide examples, hints, and test-style practice that mirrors the curriculum so they feel confident submitting independently.
What grades/subjects do you cover?
Grades 6–12 math (fractions to advanced functions) and science (biology, chemistry, physics). EQAO and exam prep available with timed drills and feedback.
Do you offer progress tracking?
Yes. We maintain a simple skills checklist and a marks trend so families can see growth over time and where to focus next.
Do you give homework?
Yes—targeted practice between sessions to lock in skills.
How do payments work?
e‑Transfer after each session or monthly bundle.
Can you help with school assignments?
We guide understanding and strategy—students always submit their own work.
